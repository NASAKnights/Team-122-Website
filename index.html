What is the FIRST Robotics Competition?

The FIRST Robotics Competition is a national high-school level event held every year, where teams of students come together to compete with their custom built robots in a unique game, which is different for each season. Teams are competing with and against each other, showing off technical skills used in the field of robotics, including but not limited to, mecahnical design, electrical engineering, programming, graphic design, social marketing and strategy.

FIRST highly emphasizes the concept of Cooperatition and Gracious Professionalism, and students are taught that competition and mutual gain are not separate notions.Gracious professionals learn and compete like crazy but treat one another with respect and kindness in the process. They avoid treating anyone like losers. No chest thumping tough talk, but no sticky-sweet platitudes either. Knowledge, competition, and empathy are comfortably blended.

Who are We?

We are The NASA Knights, FRC team 122, a community-based robotics team operating in Hampton, VA.

The team was founded in 1997 by NASA Langley Research Center, starting in a small shop closet before moving to our current location.

Students on the team are a mix of public, private and home-schooled, coming in with a variety of skills and knowledge.
