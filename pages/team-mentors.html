Mentors

Who are we?

Mentors are an essential part of the FRC pipeline. All mentors are volunteers, with a few of them having been through the FRC pipeline themselves.

Mentors are comprised of local professionals, with a breath of knowledge from different fields. The mentors on the team usually come in with knowledge in engineering, software development, machining, graphic design, 3D animation, and much more.

We're always looking for new mentors! If you feel like you want to contribute time to the team, we're always happy to have a chat!
